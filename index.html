About Us

The Flood Relief Management Center is a digital platform dedicated to supporting communities affected by floods by providing timely information, coordination of relief services, and emergency assistance. Our goal is to connect victims, volunteers, and relief organizations in one centralized system to ensure faster response and efficient distribution of resources. Through this platform, users can request help, access safety information, and stay updated on flood conditions and available support services. We aim to improve disaster response, reduce suffering, and help communities recover more quickly and safely during flood emergencies.
